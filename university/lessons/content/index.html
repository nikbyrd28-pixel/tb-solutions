Lesson 03 · free

Make content that sells — with AI

Content isn't about going viral. It's a machine: grab attention, build trust, drive action. Here's the simple version any local business needs — and how AI makes it 10 minutes of work.

You don't need to be a videographer or a copywriter. You need to understand what content is for — then let AI do the making.

The system

Only 3 content types matter

Forget trends. Every local business needs exactly three kinds of content, on repeat:

① Attention — hooks & short videos that stop the scroll ("One thing killing your sales…"). ② Trust — reviews, before/afters, testimonials, "meet the owner." ③ Action — offers, loyalty, "book now" — the reason to buy today.
The formula

The anatomy of a post that sells

Every piece follows the same shape. Miss the first 3 seconds and nothing else matters.

Hook (3 sec — a bold line or question) → One idea (don't cram) → CTA (tell them exactly what to do next).
The tool

Let AI make it — in minutes

This is where the toolstack earns its keep. Type an idea → the AI writes the script → you get a finished vertical video, a click-winning thumbnail, and a caption. No editor, no experience.

Do it right now in the free Content Studio. Type one idea and watch it become a reel + thumbnail + post.
The workflow

Batch a week in an hour

Don't make content daily — batch it. Sit down once: list 5 ideas (one attention, two trust, two action), run each through the Studio, and you've got a week done. Consistency beats perfection.

The move

Make proof for a real business

Remember the client-getting cheat code from Lesson 1? This is it. Pick a real local business in your niche, make them 2–3 pieces of content free, and walk in with finished work. That's how you turn "beginner" into "already made you something."
