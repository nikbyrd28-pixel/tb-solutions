Questions

Good to know

Do my customers need to download an app?

No. Loop lives in the browser — they scan a QR code and their card is on their phone instantly. Nothing to install, ever.

Do I need special hardware or a POS integration?

None. Your staff scan a customer's card with any phone. No scanner gun, no tablet, no POS hookup required.

Can I control the prizes and odds?

Yes — you set the reward, the point goal, the prize list and how often the grand prize hits. The odds run on the server so it can't be gamed.

How does booking work?

Members tap "Book your next visit," pick a time, and it drops straight into your customer list as a booking request for you to confirm.

How fast can I launch?

Usually within a day. We set up your branded card, prizes and poster, and you're live.
