Privacy Policy

Effective July 20, 2026

TB Solutions ("we", "us") is a digital marketing studio based in Chester County, Pennsylvania, operating tbsol.net and related tools (client portals, CRMs, lead capture pages). This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Information you give us — name, email, phone, business name, and project details when you submit a form, request a quote, create an account, book a call, or file a support request.
  • Lead information — when you submit a form on a page we operate for one of our business clients (for example, a booking or quote page), we collect the details you enter and share them with that business so they can respond to you.
  • Usage information — basic, non-identifying analytics such as pages viewed, referrer, and screen size. We also log technical errors to keep the site working.
  • Local storage — our apps use browser storage to keep you logged in and remember preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies on this site.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry, provide quotes, and deliver services you request.
  • To operate client accounts, portals, and CRMs.
  • To send service messages and, where you'd expect it, follow-ups about your inquiry. You can opt out of marketing messages at any time by replying or contacting us.
  • To improve and secure our services.

We do not sell your personal information.

Who we share it with

Service providers that host and power our tools: Supabase (database and authentication), Vercel (website hosting), Google (email delivery, calendars, AI processing of form content), and our automation server (Hostinger). If you submitted a form on a page we run for a business client, that business receives your submission. We may disclose information if required by law.

AI processing

Some form submissions are processed by AI services (such as Google Gemini) to help route, summarize, or respond to them. This content is not used by us to train AI models.

How long we keep it

We keep information for as long as needed to provide services and meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymize it.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and the CCPA), you may have additional rights. To exercise any of them, email nikbyrd28@gmail.com — we'll respond within 30 days. We do not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit, stored with row-level access controls, and accessible only to authorized accounts. No system is perfectly secure — if we learn of a breach affecting you, we'll notify you as required by law.

Children

Our services are for businesses and adults; we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We'll post any changes here and update the effective date above.
