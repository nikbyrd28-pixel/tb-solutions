Questions

Straight answers

What is an ambassador program?

It turns your happy customers into referrers. Each one gets a personal link to share; when a friend joins or buys, the customer earns a reward and you get a new customer — all tracked automatically.

Do customers need an app?

No. They open a link in their browser, share it by text or social, and see their referrals and rewards on any phone. Nothing to download.

What rewards can I offer?

Whatever fits your business — a free item, store credit, a discount, or cash. You set the reward and how many referrals it takes to earn it.

How is this different from loyalty?

Loyalty (Loop) rewards a customer for coming back. Ambassadors rewards them for bringing new people in. They work great together — keep the customers you have and multiply them.
